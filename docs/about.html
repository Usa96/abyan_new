Who we are

Abyan Building Construction Company, a prominent player in Kuwait's construction industry, is dedicated to pioneering advanced construction technologies. As the exclusive distributor and operator of COBOD 3D construction printers, Abyan is at the forefront of innovation, possessing the world’s largest BOD2 printer. This cutting-edge technology enables Abyan to transform traditional construction practices by enhancing efficiency, reducing costs, and promoting sustainability. Their notable projects include the world’s first on-site 3D printed large water tanks, demonstrating significant material savings and environmental benefits. Committed to revolutionising the construction landscape, Abyan continues to lead with innovative solutions and a focus on quality and sustainability.
